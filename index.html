Biography

Dr. Lucy Jepchoge Rono holds a PhD in Finance from Moi University, she is an associate professor in the Department of Accounting and Finance, School of Business and Economics at Moi University. She has been teaching and researching in the department for a span of 15 years. Her research focus is on savings, retirement, and investment management. She has served in leadership positions including chair of the department, Associate Dean, as well as Dean of the Business School of Moi University between 2014-2017. She is a member of the Kenya Institute of Management.
